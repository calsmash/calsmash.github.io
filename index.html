How Segno Works

How Segno Works

View nearby live music events on a map...

...In a single click.

Segno uses your current location to provide you with a bespoke map of music events, displayed relative to where you are. Don't fancy going to see a live music event today? Click next, and Segno will load the next days set of music events directly onto your map.

Or....As a list if you prefer

Each live music event is listed relative to your location, detailing how far away you are from each event. Search through music events, or click an individual event to view the event details.

View details of each music event

Including the musician lineup.

Segno provides you with all the details you could need regarding each music event, including the date and time, the venue address details and the artist lineup.

Listen to artists, before you attend.

Dedicated music streaming from SoundCloud

Segno streams songs directly to your phone for each artist listed on the event lineup, allowing you to listen to the artists songs before you attend the event, giving you the power of choice.

Submit your own music events

Segno will promote them to others.

Are you an artist playing a gig or a promoter putting on live music events? Segno gives you the power to submit your live music events and automatically promote them to other users in a few simple clicks.

Social sharing

Facebook, Twitter, SMS, Email

Segno provides you with the ability to share your favourite events on social media websites such as Facebook and Twitter. Need to tell a friend immediately about an event? Simply share the event as a text message or an email.

Keep track of things

Favourites ensures you never miss an event!

Segno allows you to add events, venues or artists to your personal favourites list. Can't decide which event you want to go to? Favourites allows you to keep track of multiple events to return to at a later date.
